IV. Essential Tips for Singapore Visitors

1. **Customs Efficiency**: In 2026, we highly recommend using the MACS 2.0 or QR Code clearance at JB CIQ to reduce queue times by up to 40%.

2. **Transport**: Stick to **Grab** or **InDrive**. Avoid illegal street taxis, especially when heading to private apartments. Simply search for the apartment name (e.g., R&F Princess Cove) in the app.

3. **Payment Safety**: Most individual studios in JB are **Cash Only** or TNG eWallet. **NEVER** pay a deposit or "insurance fee" before meeting. If someone asks for money upfront, it is 100% a scam.
